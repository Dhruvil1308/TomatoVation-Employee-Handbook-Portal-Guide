# TomatoVation Employee Portal - Quick Start Guide

Welcome to the TomatoVation Employee Portal! This guide will help you understand how to use the portal for your daily work, including tracking your attendance and managing your tasks.

## 1. Getting Started
- **Login:** Use your provided employee email (e.g., `testuser12345@gmail.com`) and your secure password to log in to the portal.
- **Dashboard:** Once logged in, you will see your personalized dashboard greeting you by your full name. Here you can get a quick overview of your day.

## 2. Tracking Your Attendance
We use a simple Clock In/Clock Out system to manage your daily hours.

- **Clocking In:** When you begin your workday, click the **"Clock In"** button on your dashboard.
- **Clocking Out:** When you finish your workday, click the **"Clock Out"** button.
- **Forgot to Clock Out?** Don't worry! If you forget to clock out, our system will automatically clock you out at **11:59 PM** to ensure your next day's logs stay clean. You'll see an "AUTO" badge next to these logs.
- **Attendance History:** Navigate to the **Attendance** tab in the menu to view all your past clock-ins, clock-outs, and total hours for the week.

## 3. Managing Your Tasks
Your assigned work will appear in the **Tasks** section.

- **Viewing Tasks:** Click on the Tasks tab to see your To-Do list.
- **Reading Long Descriptions:** If a task has a long description, click "Read More" to open a detailed view without cluttering your screen.
- **Submitting Work:** When you finish a task, you can submit it directly through the portal! Click "Submit", provide any necessary notes, and attach relevant reference links. 
- **Approval:** Once submitted, your managers (Admins/Co-Admins) will review your work and mark it as "Completed".

## 4. Tips for a Smooth Experience
- **Keep it Open:** You can leave the portal open in a browser tab throughout the day.
- **Responsive:** The portal works great on both your computer and your mobile phone!
- **Dark/Light Mode:** The portal features a beautiful, modern design. Feel free to use the theme toggle if one is available to switch between light and dark modes depending on your preference.

If you have any issues logging in or tracking your tasks, please contact your administrative team. Have a productive day!
